Geolocation

Information on client's location, based on ip

Notes:

  • Relies on two free APIs, l2.io for client IP, and freegeoip.net for location.
  • For a more stable and accurate solution, maxmind.com is not free, but well regarded.
  • Limited to 10,000 calls per hour per parent url (e.g. all apps.urban.org calls)
